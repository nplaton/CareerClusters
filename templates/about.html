CareerClusters
How to cluster job postings
>>>>>
Each day, CareerClusters scrapes sites like Indeed, GlassDoor, and StackOverflow Careers to find active job Postings. The text from these posts are clustered using the k-means clustering algorithm to find which kinds of data science jobs are being advertised on the web. Click on a topic to start a search or enter your own resume for a custom search.
How to match a resume to the best jobs
>>>
To allow users to enter any keywords or an entire resume, we needed a custom search engine. It works by vectorizing your search with tf-idf (the same way we vectorize the job postings), and comparing all vectors to your search using cosine similarity. This tool is a great way to find jobs you might have missed from all over the web.
How to optimize your resume
>
The resume optimizer compares the text in your resume with the requirements listed in a given job posting. Many companies parse incoming applications algorithmically before they even give them a chance. Use this tool to make sure your resume beats the computer and reaches a human.
